About Us

The prElearning educational system project began on March 18, 2025, and was fully completed in June 2025 as part of a special internship project by Ayu Nabihah Mohamad Yusni, a Bachelor of Science (Hons.) Information Systems Management student from Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), who started her internship at Vast Group Sdn. Bhd. on March 4, 2025.

This learning system was specially developed for 4-year-old students at Aster International Preschool, aimed at supporting a more interactive, enjoyable, and age-appropriate teaching and learning process during the early years of education.

The system was successfully built with guidance and feedback from teachers, in alignment with the curriculum implemented at the preschool.
